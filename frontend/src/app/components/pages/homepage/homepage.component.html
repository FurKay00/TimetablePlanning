This platform simulates a schedule planner within a university setup. You can view this application through the eyes of three roles:

  • Secretary:
    The secretaries oversee a bunch of classes from a faculty and plan/update their respective schedule.
  • Lecturer:
    Each lecturer has access to their own schedule and may also create/update their own personal appointments to indicate the students or the secretaries, that they are not available on certain times.
  • Student:
    The students have access to their own schedule.

Furthermore, every role can search schedules by room or lecturer.

How to select a Role?

Use the button above the navigation bar, to select an existing account. You can find the respective role behind the name in parenthesis. After selecting account, the site will automatically refresh with the selected view.

Tips for Navigating

Use the left-hand menu for the main sections. The toolbar at the top has additional quick actions like setting the view mode and filtering data.
